DIARY OF A MAD BLACK WOMAN

Title: Diary Of A Mad Black Woman

Age Restitons: PG

Actors and Actress: Kimberly Elise as Helen Simmons-McCarter, an unemployed woman. Steve Harris as Charles McCarter, a successful lawyer. Shemar Moore as Orlando, a moving truck driver. Cicely Tyson as Myrtle Simmons, the mother of Helen. Tyler Perry as: Mabel "Madea" Simmons, a tough old lady. Joe Simmons, the brother of Madea. Brian Simmons, a lawyer who is the nephew of Madea and the son of Joe. Lisa Marcos as Brenda Marcos, Charles' mistress. Tamara Taylor as Debrah, the drug-addicted wife of Brian. Tiffany Evans as Tiffany, the daughter of Brian. Gary Anthony Sturgis as Jamison Milton Jackson, a career criminal. Tamela Mann as Cora Simmons, the daughter of Madea. Chandra Currelley as herself (Chandra the club singer) Judge Mablean Ephriam as herself

A brief synopsis of the movie: Diary of a Mad Black Woman is a 2005 romantic comedy-drama,film written by Tyler Perry, which was inspired by the play of the same name. It is Perry's debut feature film, and the first entry in the Tyler Perry film franchise with this film also starring Kimberly Elise, Steve Harris, Shemar Moore, and Cecily Tyson. Directed by Darren Grant, the film was released in the US on February 25, 2005. It is the only Tyler Perry scripted film not directed by Tyler Perry. It was filmed almost entirely in Fairburn, Georgia.The sequel, Madea's Family Reunion, was released on February 24, 2006.
